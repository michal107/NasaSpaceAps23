DEPATRURE REGULATIONS

Due to the conditions in space, the length of the trip and the difficult access to medical care, the following are not allowed to participate in our tours:

  • Children under 18 years of age,
  • Pregnant women,
  • Chronically ill persons whose condition requires observation by a doctor,
  • Persons who do not obtain a flight declaration from a space flight doctor.
