A Galilean adventure

Locations: Jupiter, Io, Europe, Ganymede, and Callisto

Rediscover the neighborhood of the King of Planets! During this tour, you will have the opportunity to land on the moons Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto. We provide views such as making a snowman out of freshly fallen sulfur on Io, the seafloor of Europa, impact craters on Ganymede and Jupiter admired from a hotel on Callisto, placed at a safe distance from the Gas Giant.

In the footsteps of Voyager 2

Locations: Jupiter, Moon Io, Saturn, Titan, Uranus, Neptune

This is the route taken by an unmanned space probe sent by NASA in 1977. During our expedition, we will visit as many as 4 planets of the Solar System. We will delve into the history of the Voyager mission, but also other fascinating missions from the second half of the 20th century.

On the trail of the robotic explorers of Mars

Locations: Mars

Join us for a tour of our scientific heritage! During this space expedition, you will have the opportunity to see such historic sites as the landing areas of the Viking mission, the route of the Opportunity, Spirit, Curiosity, and Perseverance rovers, or see the site of the first manned landing on Mars!

Hot climates in the solar system

Locations: Venus

This excursion is primarily aimed at lovers of incredible experiences and tropical climates. On this trip you will visit the planets Mercury and Venus, where the extreme temperatures will have everyone spellbound! You will have the opportunity to spend time in Hotels unique to the entire solar system - a wandering hotel on Mercury and a flying city in the higher layers of Venus' atmosphere.
